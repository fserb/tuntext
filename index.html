TunText helps you read in other languages. I kid you not.

Paste a text on the box and press go

Take it with you for all sites!

1. Drag to your bookmark bar: tuntext.

2. Select a text in any page.

3. Click on it and ta-da!

Go on, try it.

If something is missing, let me know.

You can press on a word or select a phrase to get its translation.

You can also middle-click to open another dictionary on a new tab.

If there's no specialized dictionary, you go to Google images.
